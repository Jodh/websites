Party 1

Vincent

A human bard who just came into his powers due to his innate white dragon bloodline. He is a measured and cautious individual and an expert strategist and team player. He makes sure that his party survive every encounter and no one is left behind. He loves the art of story telling and incorporates personal experiences into his performances. His signature move is using whatever is at his disposal and never sticking to a single weapon or spell.

Takara

A kind hearted but brave rogue that likes to stay out of trouble and hone her jewel crafting and appraising skills. She dual wields her shortsword and dagger in battle with deadly precision, only going in for kills when the right opportunity arrives. Her signature move is striking with two blades from the blind spot of her enemies.

Ziggraut

A curious and easy going loxodont Paladin that cares about the safety of his party. Ziggraut is down to try anything once. His love for his friends is only rivalled by his love for money and glory. He is an ex-gladiator out for gold and accolades. His signature move is punching his enemies with divinely empowered brass knuckles.

Grey Spots

A tabaxi ranger whoes arrows always manage to find thier way into enemy skulls. His signatyre movie is hunter's mark followed by a longbow strike. He does not talk much and is all too ready to kill people and monsters alike if he deems them evil. He would have been a paladin of vengence if he was not a ranger.

Nifl'ah Laern

This tabaxi monk is ready to run into battle and more than willing to sacrifice himself so that his party can survive when things get tough. He uses his sharp claws and honed reflexes in the heat of battle. His signature move is bonking enemies with his quarterstaff and swiping them with a claw.
