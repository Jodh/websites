Dragon's Hiest: Party 1

Mishka

Mishka is a halfling with a chip on her shoulder. using her past as motivation to lash out on the world. she swings her massive sword to slay her foes.

Snacks

Snacks is food motivated, a self employed Kenku Bard, and minor annoyance among the artists of Waterdeep. Unwilling to learn musical theory herself, Snacks hops between inns performing songs she hears up along the way, sometimes performing them right after the original artist plays them. Snacks does not understand copyright law or observe it, and is incapable of shame.
